ABOUT SRI LANKA EVISA

Sri Lanka eVisa is an Electronic Travel Authorization to enter srilanka. Sri Lanka offers evisa for more than 100+ countries. Travelers can aply for evisa online and recieve electronic travel authrozation in PDF format. Upon arrival in Sri Lanka travelers can show the printed copy of the visa letter and get their passport stamped. Sri Lanka Goverment offers eVisa for Tourism & Business Purpose. The visa type, visa duration & number of entry depends on nationality of the applicants and fixed. Applicants are advised to obtain Sri Lanka evisa online before travelling to srilanka. E-Visa process is completly online and no paperwork is required. Once Sri Lanka e-visa is approved it will be sent via email. Applicants have to carry a printed copy of the approved e-visa letter and passport while travelling. Visa letter will be verified by Sri Lanka officer at the airport and passport will be stamped.
